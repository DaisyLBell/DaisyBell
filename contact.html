Hi my name is Daisy Bell!

I am a digital designer who attended the University of Southern California's School of Cinematic Arts. There, I majored in the Media Arts + Practice program, an interdisplinary program created by George Lucas that develops the next generation of mulltimedia creators along the intersections of design, technology, and entertainment. The program covers topics from semiotics in media to creative coding. It pushes students to consider the ways they can improve the world through creativity. In addition to Media Arts + Practice, I had the privilege to enroll in the product design program at the Ioving Young Academy, an entrepreneurial school founded by Dr. Dre and Jimmy Iovine that bridges business, design, and technology. Through the product design program, I learned to find new human-based solutions to age-old problems. Overall, my design experiences have shaped the way I view the world. It has made me an action-oriented person focused on solutions rather than obstacles.

Outside of school, I have been a creative leader and worked in professional design environments at the GEN-ZiNE, The Snowflake Project, and NewFilmmakers LA.
