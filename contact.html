Hi! My name is Daisy Bell.

I am a designer currently located in Los Angeles, CA interested in the intersections of human-centered design and data visualization.

I had the privilege to attend the University of Southern California. There, I enrolled in the Media Arts and Practice program at the School of Cinematic Arts. Courses in this program cover topics from graphic design and videography to audio design, 3D modeling, and coding.

While at USC, I also had the opportunity to join the Iovine Young Academy's product design program. While I learned many software-based skills through SCA, this program opened my eyes to human-centered design and collaboration. All of these skills helped guide my leadership at GEN-ZiNE as Creative Director.

In my free-time, I enjoy finding new ways to be sustainable, crocheting, and spending time in the great outdoors.
