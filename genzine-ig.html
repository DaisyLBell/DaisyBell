BRANDING GUIDELINES
PROCESS

1. Section editors approve articles to be published to the GEN-ZINE website.

2. The Creative Director, Editor in Chief, and Social Media Coordinator select two articles to be featured on the Instagram.

3. Social media designers select which article they would like to create a graphic for, which is due within two weeks.

4. The social media designers send rough drafts of their graphics to Creative Director for feedback.

5. Creative Director recommends edits, sometimes referring to the Social Media Team’s moodboard for the semester.

6. The social media designers edit graphics based on the feedback. If slammed with schoolwork, they can request that the Creative Director make these edits for them.

7. The final versions of the graphics are uploaded to the organization’s shared drive and posted to Instagram by the Social Media Coordinator.
