BRANDING GUIDELINES
PROCESS

1. Meet with company to understand main branding priorities and its technological limitations once my involvement with the company ends.

2. Create moodboard and color palette that best fit the company’s branding priorities. These are presented for company approval.

3. Draw initial visualization of the branding graphics.

4. Render drawings into rough draft designs. These are presented to the client for feedback.

5. Edit designs based on client feedback, relating the changes back to the original branding priorities.

6. Send final version of the graphics to the client.
