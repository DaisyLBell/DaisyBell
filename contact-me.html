A LITTLE ABOUT ME
Hi, my name is Daisy.
I am a multidisciplinary designer located in
Los Angeles, CA.

Growing up across the Puget Sound from Seattle on Bainbridge Island, I developed a deep appreciation for the environment and community. My early exposure to creativity in this island bedroom community inspired me to join an interdisciplinary design, technology, and entertainment program founded by George Lucas at USC’s School of Cinematic Arts. Through this program, I broadened my understanding of visual communication and developed numerous projects by adapting to new technology. I was so intrigued by new technology, I joined the Product Design program at Dr. Dre and Jimmy Iovine’s academy of business, technology, and design. My university education married these two programs. This curriculum, alongside my own personal adversity, developed who I am as a designer. They helped me see that design is only as powerful as the impact it makes. I hope to use my knowledge of design to empower and support as many people as possible.

My design experience encompasses 2D and 3D products, working on my own, for clients, and managing a team. A naturally hardworking and compassionate person, I enjoy collaborating on teams and developing products that entertain and astound customers.

I hope this segment shed some light on who I am and what inspires me. If you would like, I can be reached by email below.

I look forward to hearing from you. :)

Best,
Daisy
